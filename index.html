FAQ

1. What is a coworking space?
A coworking space is a shared office environment where individuals and teams can work independently or collaboratively. It’s ideal for freelancers, startups, remote workers, and businesses looking for a flexible and professional workspace.
2. What types of plans do you offer?
We offer flexible options including hot desks, dedicated desks, private offices, meeting room rentals, and virtual office packages. You can choose hourly, daily, monthly, or custom plans based on your needs.
3. Can I book a space for just one day?
Yes! We offer day passes that give you full access to our coworking space and amenities for the day.
4. Do you offer high-speed internet?
Absolutely. All our members enjoy fast, secure, and reliable Wi-Fi throughout the workspace.
5. Are meeting rooms included in the membership?
Some plans include a set number of meeting room hours each month. Additional time can be booked at member-discounted rates.
